Depressive Mac and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • Boxed Kraft Mac and Cheese (or any other boxed mac and cheese, really)
  • Water
  • Yellow onion
  • Butter
  • Milk

Instructions:

  1. Chop up your onion into thin slices
  2. Add butter to a pan and add the onion slices. Wait until they are golden brown
  3. Set aside your onions and prepare mac and cheese following the box instructions
  4. Mix and everything together
  5. Serve and enjoy your unhealthy comfort dinner
