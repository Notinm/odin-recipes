Motivational Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • A banana
  • All purpose flour
  • An egg

Instructions:

  1. With a fork, smash the banana on a bowl
  2. Crack your egg over the smashed banana and add a tablespoon of flour
  3. Mix everything together
  4. On a high heat pan, pour your batter. Wait a few seconds and flip your pancake
  5. After failing miserably and getting some bits stuck to the pan, serve your nasty-ass pancake
